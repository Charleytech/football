Tournament Rules & Regulations

TOURNAMENT RULES & REGULATIONS 8-Team Football Tournament (or League Format Based on Participation)

TOURNAMENT FORMAT

      A. Standard Format (8 Teams)
      • The tournament shall consist of 8 registered teams if available, divided into two groups (Group A & Group B) of 4 teams each.
      • Each group plays in a single round-robin format (each team plays the other 3 in their group once).
      • The top 2 teams from each group qualify for the semi-finals.
      • Semi-final winners meet in the final to determine the champion. B. Alternative Format (Less than 8 Teams)
      • If fewer than 8 teams register, the tournament will switch to a league format. • All teams will play against each other once (single round-robin). • Points will be awarded as follows:
      o Win: 3 points
      o Draw: 1 point
      o Loss: 0 points
      • The team with the highest points at the end of all matches will be declared the winner.
      • In case of a tie-on points, tie-breaking rules will apply.


      2. MATCH RULES
      • Match Duration: 2 halves of 45 minutes with a 15-minute halftime break. • Group & League Stage Draws: Allowed (1 point to each team). • Knockout Stage (Semi-final/Final):
      o If tied at full time, extra time of 10 minutes (5 per half) will be played.
      o If still tied, the match proceeds to a penalty shootout (best of 5, then sudden death).


      3. PLAYER REGISTRATION & ELIGIBILITY
      • Each team must register a maximum of 23 players and up to 3 officials (e.g., coach, medic).
      • A minimum of 7 players is required to start or continue a match. • Players must be registered before the tournament begins.
      • Players must present valid ID.
      • A player may only play for one team during the tournament.


      4. SUBSTITUTIONS
      • Each team is allowed up to 5 substitutions per match.
      • Substitutions must be made during a stoppage with referee permission. • Maximum 3 substitution windows per team, excluding halftime.


      5. YELLOW & RED CARD RULES
      Yellow Cards
      • A player who receives 2 yellow cards in different matches is suspended for the next match.
      • All yellow cards are reset after the group or league stage.
      • Fine for a yellow card is N500
      Red Cards
      • A direct red card results in a minimum one-match suspension. • Further sanctions may apply depending on the offense.
      • A player with two red cards in the tournament is disqualified.
      • Fine for a red card is N1000


      6. EQUIPMENT & KITS
      • All players must wear proper football kits with visible shirt numbers.
      • Teams must have both primary and alternate jerseys to avoid color clashes. • Shin guards are mandatory.
      • Only FIFA-standard footballs will be used.


      7. TIE-BREAKING RULES
      Applicable in both group and league formats when teams are level on points:
      1. Goal difference
      2. Goals scored
      3. Head-to-head result
      4. Fair play record (fewer yellow/red cards)
      5. Drawing of lots by the organizing committee


      8. DISCIPLINE & CONDUCT
      • Referees’ decisions are final.
      • Aggressive behaviour, threats, or misconduct may lead to player or team disqualification.
      • All participants must show sportsmanship and respect.


      9. PROTESTS & APPEALS
      • Protests must be filed in writing within 30 minutes after a match. • A disciplinary panel will rule within 24 hours.
      • Protests referee decisions will not be entertained unless there is evidence of misconduct.


      10. PUNCTUALITY
      • Teams must report 30 minutes before kick-off.
      • A team arriving more than 15 minutes late forfeits the match (3–0 loss). • If both teams are absent or late, both may face disqualification.


      11. AWARDS
      • Champion Trophy & Medals for winners and runner-up. • Optional awards:
      o Golden Boot – Top Scorer
      o Golden Glove – Best Goalkeeper
      o Most Valuable Player (MVP)
      o Fair Play Team Award
